T: Permissiveness Tiers

T0 Total Prohibition

AI use is banned for all relevant work.

Triggers: "Strictly prohibited", "No AI", "AI is plagiarism".

T1 Default Prohibited

Not allowed unless explicitly permitted for a specific assignment.

Triggers: "Only in assignments where permitted", "Except when instructed".

T2 Permission Gated

Requires prior instructor approval on a case-by-case basis.

Triggers: "Ask me first", "Requires prior permission", "Consult instructor".

T3 Bounded Use

Allowed for support (brainstorming, outlining) but not for drafting.

Triggers: "May use to brainstorm", "Support learning", "No AI-generated submissions".

T4 Collaborative

Active use encouraged; treated as a partner or co-author.

Triggers: "Use to enhance work", "Collaborate with AI", "AI as a thought partner".

T5 Mandatory

Assignments require the use of AI to be completed.

Triggers: "You must use AI", "Required AI workflow".
