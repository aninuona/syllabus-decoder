Policy Guide

Click a card to see the definition for each classification.

T: Permissiveness Tiers

T0 Total Prohibition

AI use is banned for all work. "Strictly prohibited," "No AI allowed."

T1 Default Prohibited

Not allowed unless explicitly permitted for a specific assignment.

T2 Permission-Gated

Requires prior instructor approval case-by-case.

T3 Bounded Use

Allowed for support (brainstorming, tutoring) but not for drafting work.

T4 Collaborative

Active use encouraged; AI treated as a partner or co-author.

T5 Mandatory

Assignments require the use of AI to be completed.

C: Compliance Levels

C0 None

No requirement to disclose or cite AI use.

C1 Minimal

Mention AI use, but no specific format required.

C2 Structured

Formal citation (APA/MLA) and specific attribution of AI's role.

C3 Full Audit

Requires submission of prompt logs, drafts, and reflections.

E: Enforcement Posture

E0 Trust-Based

Relies on student integrity; no mention of monitoring.

E1 Instructive

Warning about hallucinations and risks of academic dishonesty.

E2 Investigative

Explicit mention that AI detectors or audits will be used.

E3 Zero-Tolerance

Automatic referral to student conduct for any suspected violation.
